AJ is the Assistant Principal of Academics at St. John Paul II School, High School Division, in Hyannis, MA.

AJ has over a decade of experience teaching history, U.S. government, and research in high school and college classrooms. In his current position, he is responsible for managing academic life for 250+ students each year, observing and offering feedback to 20+ teachers, developing curriculum in social studies and organizing vertical alignment of curriculum in all disciplines, and leading local professional development.

AJ is working to become a full-stack developer through Codecademy and other sites. He will be teacher AP Computer Science Principles during the upcoming school year. All content on this site was coded by AJ. Please send me feedback!

He holds a Ph.D. in U.S. History from Boston University, an M.A. in U.S. History from the University of Colorado at Boulder, a Master of Theological Studies degree from the Iliff School of Theology in Denver, and a B.A. in Religion from Maryville College.
